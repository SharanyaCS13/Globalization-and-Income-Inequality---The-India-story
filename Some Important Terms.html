Some Important Terms


KOF Index: The KOF Index of Globalization is an index of the degree of globalization of 122 countries.
Ginni Index: The Gini index, or Gini co-efficient, measures income distribution across a population.
When there is an increase in the Ginni Index, that means that there is an increase in income inequality.

ILO: A person in employment as defined by the International Labour Office (ILO)
is a person aged 15 or over who has done at least one hour's paid work in a given week,
or who is absent from work for certain reasons
(annual leave, sickness, maternity, etc.) and for a certain period of time.
